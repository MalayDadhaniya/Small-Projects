ABHIGYAN - 2023
Atmiya University is celebrating Popularization of Science activities under the umbrella of National Science Day in the name of ABHIGYAN. This year also we are celebrating ABHIGYAN-2023 from 13th to 15th February, 2023 and this time we will focus on imparting general science formulas and principles through technical (Parishram) and non-technical (Prerna) events in the form of games.
